Alcatraz Island Today

- Status: Part of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area.
- Tourism: Over 1.5 million visitors annually.
- Attractions: Prison tours, ranger-led programs, exhibits on history and ecology.
- Wildlife: Home to seabirds, gardens, and marine life.
- Cultural Impact: Symbol of isolation and resilience, featured in films, novels, and pop culture.
- Preservation: National Park Service maintains the site, balancing tourism with conservation.
